3-ingredient KitKat fudge slice

Easy 2-ingredient strawberry soft serve

Make your own dairy-free soft serve with this deliciously easy 2-ingredient strawberry and banana recipe. That is sure to be your go to sweet recipe in seconds

Ingredients

Kit Kat fudge
Only 3 ingredients needed!!

- 395g Nestlé Sweetened Condensed Milk

- 2 x 200g pkts Nestlé Baker's Choice Milk Chocolate Bits

- 3 x 185g pkts Nestlé funpack KitKat, unwrapped

Salted carmel cups
Sure to Wow your guests!

- 24 Arnott’s Choc Ripple biscuits

- 380g can Nestlé Top ‘n’ Fill Caramel

- Sea salt flakes, to serve

Strawberry and banana soft serve
Easy soft serve prepared in minutes

- 500g strawberries, hulled, plus extra halved strawberries to serve

- 2 bananas, peeled, chopped

- Extra 2 bananas

- Waffle baskets, to serve

.

How to make them

  • How to make Kit Kat Fudge

    Step 1: Grease and line the base and sides of a 20cm square cake pan with baking paper.

    Step 2: Place the sweetened condensed milk and chocolate bits in a medium-sized saucepan over low heat. Cook, stirring, for 5 to 7 minutes or until melted and combined.

    Step 3: Pour half of the chocolate mixture into the prepared pan. Place a layer of Funpack KitKat chocolate on top. Pour over remaining chocolate mixture and smooth the surface. Arrange another layer of KitKat on top. Cover with plastic wrap and place in the fridge for 2 hours or until set.

    Step 4: Cup into squares to serve.

    Enjoy!!

  • How to make Salted Carmel Cookie cups

    Step 1: Preheat oven to 180C/160C fan forced. Place a biscuit over 6 holes of a 12-hole patty pan. Bake for 2 minutes or until softened slightly. Use a teaspoon to carefully press biscuits into holes to form cups. Cool, then transfer to a wire rack. Repeat in 3 more batches.

    Step 2: Place the caramel in a bowl and whisk until smooth. Divide among the cookie cups. Place in the fridge for 30 minutes or until firm.

    Step 3: Sprinkle the cookie cups with a little salt just before serving.

    Enjoy!!

  • How to make Strawberry and Banana Soft serve

    Step 1: Place strawberries and chopped banana in a snap-lock bag. Freeze overnight.

    Step 2: Place frozen strawberries and banana in a food processor. Process until mixture looks like coarse crumbs. Set aside 1/4 cup mixture in freezer until required. Chop extra bananas. Add to processor. Process until smooth.

    Step 3: Transfer mixture to a piping bag fitted with a 1cm fluted nozzle. Pipe soft serve into waffle baskets bowls. Sprinkle with reserved strawberry crumble mixture. Serve topped with extra strawberries.

    Enjoy!!
